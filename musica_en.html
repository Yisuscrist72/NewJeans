NewJeans 1st EP 'New Jeans'

New Jeans is the debut EP by the South Korean girl group NewJeans. It was released on August 1, 2022, in digital format, and on August 8, 2022, in physical format by ADOR, a subsidiary record label of HYBE. The mini-album contains four tracks, including three main singles: "Attention", "Hype Boy", and "Cookie".

NewJeans 'OMG'

OMG is the first single album by the South Korean girl group NewJeans. It was released on January 2, 2023, by ADOR. It contains the main single of the same name and "Ditto", which was released before the single album on December 19, 2022.

NewJeans 2nd EP 'Get Up'

Get Up is the second EP by NewJeans. It was released on July 21, 2023, with "Super Shy", "ETA", and "Cool With You" as the main tracks of the EP.

Supernatural is the debut Japanese single by NewJeans. It was released on June 21, 2024, with the main track of the same name and the song "Right Now". The album was designed in collaboration with Japanese contemporary artist Takashi Murakami.

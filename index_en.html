NewJeans, a fourth-generation K-pop group

They are a girl group under ADOR. They debuted on July 22, 2022, with the lead single "Attention," before releasing their debut EP, New Jeans, on August 1, 2022.

NewJeans is known for their "girl-next-door" image and their pop and R&B songs inspired by the 1990s and 2000s, with influences from various dance and club music styles.

The "girl-next-door" concept in K-pop refers to an image or style that is approachable, natural, and relatable. This concept contrasts with more glamorous or idealized images often seen in the entertainment industry.

Origin of the name

The group's name, NewJeans, has a double meaning. On one hand, it reflects the group's goal of becoming icons of the current generation, just like jeans that never go out of style and are worn daily. On the other hand, there is the meaning of "New Genes," which sounds similar in English to the group's name, referring to the group as the beginning of a new generation of pop music.
